Bacon Fries

Description

Bacon Fries are something out of this world. French Fries wrapped with Bacon are a healthy snack which gives you an additional lifespan of 10 to 15 years!

Ingredients

  • Fries
  • Bacon
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven
  2. Wrap a bacon slice around each French Fry and place them on the baking sheet
  3. Bake them in the preheated oven until the bacon is browned and crips (should take about 30 minutes)
