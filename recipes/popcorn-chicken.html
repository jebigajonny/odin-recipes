Popcorn Chicken

Description

A bite-sized, tasty dish made out of deep-fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Eggs
  • Lemon juice
  • Oil
  • Chicken breast

Steps

  1. Mix flour, garlic powder, salt and black pepper together. Beat eggs and lemon juice together.
  2. Heat Oil in a large pot over medium heat
  3. Dredge chicken pieces in flour mixture, then dip them into the beaten egg
  4. Fry them in oil for 7 to 8 minutes until they're golden brown
